What is a home car wash?

Lavage Unique's home car wash allows you to keep your vehicle clean and cared for without having to travel to a garage or car wash centre. Whether you are in the centre of Marbella, Puerto Banús or in nearby areas such as Nueva Andalucía, our detailing experts will come to your location to offer you a personalised, quality service.
